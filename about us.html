About B.Bap KoreanFood

With a touch of modern contemporary approach, B.bap is proud to introduce a special line-up of most appetizing authentic Korean street food available right in the heart of Kuala Lumpur. By setting its name as a reflection of the new Korean Fusion restaurant that served breakfast, lunch, tea and dinner, the menu is specially designed to incorporate a wide range of wholesome popular Korean dishes which include various type of Bi Bim Bap, Noodles, Rice and Soup dishes, and assortments of popular side dishes to complement the mains. On top of that, B.bap has also taken the initiative to improvise the menu by infusing a slight kick of local flavors to several of its Korean-fusion dishes.

Situated in the busy nestling commercialized hub of Kuala Lumpur, B.bap is the ideal restaurant for customers who are seeking for quick yet delicious meals in the midst of a fast-paced lifestyle thus allowing services options of either dining in, takeaway or delivery. Whether it is for personal or group consumption, the “Take-out Menu” is made available for office folks who desire for a quick get-together lunch or dinner or even between meetings over teatime.

For Korean foodie-lover customers, they will surely be delighted to find almost all the popular Korean dishes available in B.bap. We intends to create not just specialty food, but also a contemporary modern lifestyle that is ideal to the customers in the vicinity by offering all-rounder meals whether it is for breakfast, lunch, teatime or dinner. To serve the customers better, B.bap operates daily from 10am to 10pm. For office folks, they may grab their favorite breakfast from 8am till 11am, Monday to Friday. Hungry and hurry? B.bap is definitely worth every penny spent.
